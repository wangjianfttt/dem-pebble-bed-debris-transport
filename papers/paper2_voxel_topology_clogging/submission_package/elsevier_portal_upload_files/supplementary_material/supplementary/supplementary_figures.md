## Boundary

Supplementary figures support the bounded mechanisms stated in the manuscript. They should not be used to claim pressure-calibrated clogging, a universal critical transition, or a complete source-location law.

Fig. S6 and Fig. S7 are retained as internal/historical diagnostic figures in the provenance manifest but are omitted from the formal submission catalogue because their evidence role is superseded by Fig. S8--S10.
